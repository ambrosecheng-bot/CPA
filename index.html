A1  ·  The 3 Audit Pre-Work Tasks — What We Automate & Why
Format Translation
1. TB → Proforma Financial Statements
450–750 hrs/year across 150 clients
Each client's Trial Balance — from Xero, QuickBooks, MYOB or Excel — must be manually mapped line-by-line into your Proforma FS template: reclassify accounts, merge line items, compute subtotals, insert prior-year comparatives. This is pure copy-paste. No judgement. No expertise. 3–5 hours per client, per year.
Mechanical Calculation
2. Profits Tax Computation First Draft
300–600 hrs/year across 150 clients
From the audited P&L: add back depreciation, disallowable expenses, entertainment; deduct HCA/AA; carry forward losses; apply 16.5% or 15% rate. Same structure every time. 90% of the adjustments are identical across clients. Only 10% requires genuine tax judgement — yet staff spend 100% of the time on the full computation.
Document Assembly
3. Audit Confirmation Letters
400–600 hrs/year incl. tracking & follow-up
Bank confirmations, debtor confirmations, creditor confirmations — your assistant extracts balances from the workpaper, fills each into a letter template, prints, stamps, tracks replies, chases non-responses. Every client. Every year. 100% document assembly. Zero audit content. 2–3 hours per client plus 100+ hours of annual tracking.

Combined: 1,150–1,950 hours per year. Zero professional judgement required. 100% automatable.
